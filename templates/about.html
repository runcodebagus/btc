Information


In this Bitcoin price prediction system, the target value being predicted is the Closing Price. The Closing Price is considered the most important value in technical analysis, as it determines buy or sell signals across various technical indicators. Ultimately, this value triggers investors to take buying or selling positions as their final reaction.


The values required in this system for prediction are:
1. Opening Price: The first recorded price when trading begins for the day.
2. High Price and Low Price: The highest and lowest prices recorded during the day, reflecting the daily trading range. These values indicate the level of confidence or rationality investors have in taking buying or selling positions.
